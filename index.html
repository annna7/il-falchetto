THE IL FALCHETTO EXPERIENCE

We could tell you all about the experience of fine dining at Il Falchetto but,
however proud we may be of our establishment, we will leave our guests to speak for themselves.

Il Falchetto is hands down the best Italian restaurant in Bucharest! The ambiance is cozy and inviting, and the food is simply outstanding. The pasta dishes are perfectly cooked, and the sauces are flavorful and well-balanced. The wood-fired pizzas are some of the best I've ever had, with a thin and crispy crust that is simply divine. The seafood dishes are also not to be missed, with the freshest catch cooked to perfection. The service is attentive and friendly, making for a truly memorable dining experience. I highly recommend Il Falchetto to anyone looking for a top-notch Italian restaurant in Bucharest.

If you're in Bucharest and in the mood for some delicious Italian cuisine, look no further than Il Falchetto! The food here is simply amazing, with a menu that features classic Italian dishes made with the finest and freshest ingredients. From the antipasti to the desserts, every dish is a masterpiece, with bold and authentic flavors that will transport you straight to Italy. The wine selection is also impressive, with a variety of Italian wines that pair perfectly with the food. The service is impeccable, with a knowledgeable and friendly staff that makes you feel right at home. Il Falchetto is a true gem and a must-visit for anyone who loves great Italian food!

AN ADDICTION FOR FINE DINING

Italian and Mediterranean kitchen based on fresh fish and sea food imported directly two times per week from Mazzara del Vallo and Terracina Golf in Lazio.

The finest raw, fresh fish and seafood are prepared by our Italian chef, who is long renowned for creating the innovative dishes that have satisfied our customers for years.

Our restaurant's wine collection is truly impressive, featuring a diverse and carefully curated selection of exquisite wines that perfectly complement the delicious cuisine. With offerings from some of the world's best wine regions, it's an oenophile's dream come true!
